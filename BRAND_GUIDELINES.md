# Top Escritórios de Advocacia — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
- Domain/Logo: top.escritorio.ai
- Stylized: top (white) + .escritorio (#555) + .ai (#888)
- Full descriptive: Ranking dos Escritórios Mais Digitais do Brasil

## Typography
- Primary font: Söhne (ChatGPT's font)
- Fallback: -apple-system, Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif
- Headings: font-weight 600
- Body: font-weight 400
- No decorative or display fonts

## Color Palette
- Background: #000000
- Surface: #0a0a0a
- Border: #222222
- Border hover: #444444
- Text primary: #ffffff
- Text secondary: #888888
- Text muted: #555555
- Text dim: #444444
- No accent colors — strictly monochrome

## Design Principles
- Minimalist black and white
- No gradients, no shadows, no rounded corners
- Sharp rectangles only
- Thin 1px borders (#222)
- Maximum whitespace
- Content speaks for itself
- Inspired by ChatGPT's clean, utilitarian UI

## UI Components
- Buttons: bg-white text-black, sharp edges, no border-radius
- Cards: bg-[#0a0a0a] border border-[#222]
- Badges: border border-[#333] text-[#888], no background color
- Inputs: bg-[#0a0a0a] border border-[#222] text-white
- Links: text-white hover:text-[#ccc]

## 3D City
- Pure B&W scene on black background
- Buildings: grayscale shades (#1a1a1a to #6a6a6a)
- Verified buildings: white (#ffffff)
- Windows: white emissive
- No colored lights — white only
- Fog: black, for depth

## Spacing
- Base: 4px grid
- Section padding: 24px
- Element gaps: 8px
